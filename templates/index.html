Welcome to

A web application developed as part of a research initiative to harness the power of machine learning for fluvial flood prediction in the Municipality of Camaligan. Our mission is to support communities by providing accurate, timely, and accessible flood forecasts to help reduce risk and enhance preparedness.

Use the Flood Extent Map to explore predicted flood levels and stay informed through our integrated early warning system.
